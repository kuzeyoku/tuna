We’re permitting reform essential to advance energy infrastructure development

We are facing a supply chain crisis for the very energy & raw materials it needs to upgrade our outdated roads, bridges and transit systems, and to build new electrical cars, solar panels wind turbines. The demand for energy and minerals has quickly outpaced existing supplies & even the pipeline of coming projects. Current permitting process is cumbersome, often stalling projects for years – sometimes decades – with duplicative reviews, a burdensome approval process for the necessary investment in domestic energy.

Require federal agencies to collaborate on a single environmental review documents, for establishing a reasonable timeframe.
